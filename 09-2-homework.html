2. Heart a Designer

Here’s an odd assortment of graphic designers—or individuals who graphic designers seem to orbit—that may have something to do with our working method of programming modular bits and making variations on a theme. Pick a name on the list (and if you feel strongly about someone not on here that’s also fine). Do some light research online, or in bookshops, or even contact then through Twitter, etc. if that’s possible. Compile just enough information to chat candidly about this person and their work for about thirty seconds next week. What’s the general theme of their work? What’s your favorite work of theirs? Least favorite? Have in mind some URLs or searches so we can quickly pull up relevant images and so on.
